Install Ohmigo

Step-by-step guide to install Ohmigo using Apple TestFlight.

What is TestFlight?

TestFlight is an Apple service that enables developers to distribute beta versions of their apps to real users. It’s an essential tool both for developers who want to test their apps in real-world conditions and for users who are excited to try out new software before it hits the main market.

Why Use TestFlight?

  • Early Access: Experience features still in development before they become publicly available.
  • Influence Development: Provide valuable feedback that directly influences the app's refinement.
  • Ease of Use: Join beta tests effortlessly with just a few taps.
  • Security and Privacy: TestFlight complies with Apple's stringent privacy policies, ensuring your data is protected.

Step-by-Step Installation Guide

Step 1: Downloading TestFlight

  1. Install TestFlight: Download TestFlight from the App Store by searching for “TestFlight” or using this direct link.
  2. Open TestFlight: After installation, open TestFlight. You’ll see a welcome screen prompting you to accept an invitation or redeem a code.

Step 2: Accepting Your Invitation

  1. Receive the Invitation: You’ll get an email from Apple (via TestFlight) with an invitation link. Check your spam folder if you don’t see it.
  2. Use the Invitation Link: Tap the link, then tap "Accept" in TestFlight. If you have a code, enter it manually in the “Redeem” section.

Step 3: Installing the App

  1. Select the App: Tap the app name inside TestFlight.
  2. Install the App: Tap the “Install” button to begin downloading the app.
  3. Open and Test: Open the app from your home screen or App Library.
  4. Send Feedback: Tap "Send Feedback" in TestFlight to report bugs or suggestions.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Not Receiving an Invitation Email: Check your spam or junk mail folder, as invitation emails from TestFlight can sometimes be filtered there.
  • Invitation Link Not Working: Ensure you're opening the link on the same device where TestFlight is installed. If issues persist, try restarting your device.
  • App Not Appearing in TestFlight: Refresh the TestFlight app by pulling down on the screen. If the app still doesn't appear, confirm that you've accepted the invitation correctly.
  • Installation Errors: Ensure your device has enough storage space and is running a compatible version of iOS. Restarting your device can also help resolve installation issues.
  • Feedback Submission Problems: Make sure you are using the latest version of TestFlight. Older versions might have issues with feedback submission. Ensure that screen recording and screenshot permissions are enabled in your iOS settings.
