Ohmigo

The easiest way to decode resistor values from color bands

About the App

Ohmigo is a simple and intuitive mobile application designed to calculate the resistance of electronic resistors based on their color bands. With camera integration and manual selection, you can easily identify resistor values anytime, anywhere.
