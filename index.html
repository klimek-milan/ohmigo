Meet Ohmigo

Your smart assistant for decoding resistor color codes in seconds.

What can Ohmigo do?

  • Instantly detect resistor colors using your phone camera.
  • Accurately calculate resistance values based on color bands.
  • Support for 4-band, 5-band, and 6-band resistors.
  • Manual input option if camera detection is not available.
  • Real-time unit conversion (Ohms, KOhms, MOhms).

Ohmigo is designed for electronics enthusiasts, students, and engineers who need a quick and reliable tool for reading resistor values without manual calculations. Whether you are building circuits or studying electronics, Ohmigo saves your time and minimizes errors.
